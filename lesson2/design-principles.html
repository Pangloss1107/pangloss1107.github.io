Accessibility

Intendencia de Montevideo

intendenciademontevideo

This website belongs to a state organization. Several important procedures are carried out there, and most Uruguayans (or all) have physically gone at some point to carry out some procedure there. Currently, there are many procedures that can be managed through the website, which means that the user does not have to leave the comfort of their home to do so. As there are a variety of people of different ages who have to carry out the procedures, it is important that the website is accessible to all of them. I consider that the principle of Accessibility was correctly applied, since I believe that all the people who access this page will know where to go to find what they are looking for.
