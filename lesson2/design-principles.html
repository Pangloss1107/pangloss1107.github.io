Accessibility

Intendencia de Montevideo

intendenciademontevideo

This website belongs to a state organization. Several important procedures are carried out there, and most Uruguayans (or all) have physically gone at some point to carry out some procedure there.
Currently, there are many procedures that can be managed through the website, which means that the user does not have to leave the comfort of their home to do so.
As there are a variety of people of different ages who have to carry out the procedures, it is important that the website is accessible to all of them.
I consider that the principle of Accessibility was correctly applied, since I believe that all the people who access this page will know where to go to find what they are looking for.

Repetition

HBO Max

hbomax

In this HBO Max website, we can see that in addition to the responsive design principle, the repetition principle is used.
When browsing the page, we find that the same style of buttons, styles, fonts, navigation structure and colors are repeated.
What is sought when applying this principle is that the user who enters the page feels more familiar with the website as he explores it.

Responsive

W3Schools

w3schools

This is one of the websites I use the most when learning a new programming language, or when I'm stuck on something and need answers.
Most of the time I access this website from my notebook, but when I'm in another place and a question arises, or I just want to learn how to do something, I use my cell phone.
Personally, I think it is very important that a website like this applies the "Responsive" design principle because it allows any user who needs to use it to have a great experience doing so.
Since it will not be difficult for the person to navigate the website from any device.
